Enrollment Process

Enrollment connects a Student record to a specific Session schedule (MWF or TThS).

Step One

Identify Student

Select the student from the pre-populated dropdown list.

Step Two

Assign Schedule

Pick the time block. The branch location is automatically locked.

Step Three

Save & Sync

This triggers the Xano update and places the child on the Roster.
