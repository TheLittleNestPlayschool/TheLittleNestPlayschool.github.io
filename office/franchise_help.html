Branch Management & Enrollment Protocols

Version 2.0 • Secured

Primary Action: Enrollment Master

The Enrollment Master uses a Gated Linear Workflow. You must complete each step in order (0 through 6) to unlock the final database sync.

STEPS 1-2

Parent & Student Profiling

Use the "Toggle Search" button to switch between creating a new record or finding an existing one.

  • ● Proper Case: Names automatically standardize (e.g. "dave" ➔ "Dave").
  • ● Modify: Once saved, use the "Modify" pill to correct errors.
STEP 3

Nest Assessment

Select the evaluating teacher and date. Choose behavior/academic items from the QA grid.

Requirement

You must select at least one item to proceed. The counter tracks your progress against the 10-item standard.

STEPS 4-6

Logistics & Final Sync

Select the session slot (pre-set by Head Office), add authorized pickup persons, and set the start date.

Sync Operations

Clicking "Save Enrollment Record" performs a multi-table update. A "Registration Success" modal will confirm the student is live in the Roster.
